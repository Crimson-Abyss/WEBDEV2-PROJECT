Our Story

About Chicken Pastil

Pastil is a beloved Filipino street food originating from Mindanao. It consists of seasoned shredded chicken or other proteins served on steamed rice, all wrapped in a banana leaf for a unique, aromatic flavor.

Founded in 2023, Pastil-an brings the authentic taste of Mindanao to your table. Every piece is handcrafted with love, using traditional recipes passed down through generations.

We pride ourselves on delivering the most delicious and authentic chicken pastil, wrapped in banana leaf and cooked to perfection. A true taste of Mindanao's favorite street food!
